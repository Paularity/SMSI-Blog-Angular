About IMMS

Integrated Maintenance Management Services is an Australian company based in Perth. Our combined management team has over 100 years of mining business experience. We are an engineering project management firm with experienced and reputable business affiliates in the field of engineering and supply of parts in Australia and globally. IMMS provides service for both new projects and currently operating mining facilities.
